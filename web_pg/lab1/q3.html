Vegetable Stir Fry

This quick and healthy vegetable stir fry is packed with flavor and colorful veggies. It’s perfect for a fast weeknight meal and can be customized with your favorite vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the vegetables and stir fry for 5–7 minutes.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and mix well.
  5. Cook for another 2 minutes and serve hot.
