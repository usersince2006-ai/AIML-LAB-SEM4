🍛 Authentic Chicken Curry

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Serves: 4 people

📋 Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 large onions (finely chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

👨‍🍳 Cooking Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, turmeric, and salt. Let it marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat oil: In a large pot, heat oil over medium flame until shimmering.
  3. Sauté aromatics: Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown (about 8-10 minutes).
  4. Add spices: Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add tomatoes: Pour in tomato puree and cook until oil separates (about 5 minutes).
  6. Add curry powder: Mix in curry powder, red chili powder, and cook for 1 minute.
  7. Cook chicken: Add marinated chicken and mix well. Cook on high heat for 5 minutes.
  8. Simmer: Add 1 cup water, cover, and simmer for 25 minutes until chicken is fully cooked.
  9. Finish: Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot: Enjoy with naan bread, rice, or roti.
💡 Chef's Tip: For a creamier curry, add 2 tablespoons of cream in the final step. Adjust spice levels according to your preference!
